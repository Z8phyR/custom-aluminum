Products

We provide two types of matching products

Railing and Screen Enclosures

Mechanical Aluminum Railing

Called 'mechanical' because the component parts of this rail system are fastened together with screws. This creates a very clean look, since there are no welds and the screws are hidden. One benefit of this system is its cost effect repairs. Since it is made up of component parts, some repairs can be made without replacing the entire piece of rail.

Available in two stock colors - white and bronze - or powder coated in hundreds of colors and textures. If your project is large enough, we can special order our materials in your preferred color

Screen Enclosures

More Pictures

We offer Florida style screen enclosures with aluminum frames and coated fiberglass screen. Each panel is custom made to fit your openings then assembled and installed on site. The screen is installed after the frames to ensure the screen is taught. This Florida screen system allows us to span much greater distances, up to 15 feet in some cases, with no posts to obstruct your view.

As you can see from the picture, our screen system matches exactly with our railing system for a uniform look.

Give us a call. We are happy to consult with you to determine if we have a product that will fit your needs.
