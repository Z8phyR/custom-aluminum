Things you should know

For over seven years Custom Aluminum was pretty much behind the scenes in Wichita. Offering our products and services primarily through contractors and sub-contractors. Some selling our products and services as thier own while we did ALL the fabrication AND installation for them. The only way to find us directly was through word of mouth.
From 2001-07 we were the sole local supplier of mechanical aluminum railing in the Wichita area and continue to be, by far, the most experienced and professional. When choosing a company, you should want to know how long they have actually been fabricating and installing aluminum railing, not just how long they have been selling our products and services under their name.
You will also be interested in the source of their materials and whether that company stands behind their products...does their supplier provide a warranty? Not just the local company that you know, but the one that supplies them, because let's face it, in this economic climate your local deck guy might not be around tomorrow to stand behind his work. Our supplier is a strong company, still growing, even in this economy. They stand behind their products with a 10 year warranty.
If you are considering remodeling your deck or putting up a new one and would like to use our railing or a screen enclosure, you may be wondering how this will work. Will you have to hire separate companies, one for the deck and then us for the railing or screens. Not necessarliy. If you have a contractor in mind that you trust to build your deck, we will meet with them to ensure our designs will work together. An even easier solution is to hire one of our approved contractors. They can give you one estimate for the entire job.
Approved Contractors: Group Construction - 316-655-6960 We have worked with these guys for years. If we were to hire someone to build a deck, it would be them. They are honest, no-nonsense, consistent and professional.

Give us a call. We are happy to consult with you to determine if we have a product that will fit your needs.
